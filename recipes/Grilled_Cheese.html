Grilled Cheese Recipe that BUSSES

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich. DO IT!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

  1. Step 1

    Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat & butter one side of a slice of bread

  2. Step 2

    Add 1 slice of cheese and cook until lightly broned on one side. Then flip over and cook until cheese is melted

  3. Step 3

    Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese
